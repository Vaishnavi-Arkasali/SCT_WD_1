Welcome to Haveri

Haveri is a vibrant city in Karnataka, India, known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and famous Byadagi red chillies. Explore its temples, educational institutions, and unique traditions.

Explore Attractions

Tourist Attractions

Siddheshwara Temple

A renowned temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its beautiful architecture and spiritual significance.

Hukkeri Math

An important religious and cultural center, attracting devotees and visitors from across the region.

Bada (Birthplace of Kanakadasa)

The birthplace of the famous poet and saint Kanakadasa, a site of historical and cultural importance.

About Haveri

Haveri is the administrative headquarters of Haveri district in Karnataka. The city is famous for its cardamom garlands, Byadagi red chillies, and a rich legacy of art and literature. It is home to several educational institutions, including engineering and medical colleges, and is a gateway to many historical temples and monuments.
